Comes loaded as a full boxing match.

12 rounds. 3 minutes work. 1 minute rest. Open the app and that's what you get — a real boxing setup, ready to go. Adjust anything. Hit start. Put your phone down.

THE BELL RUNS THE SHOW
Round start, round end, break end. A warning bell fires before each round closes so you're never caught mid-combination. Choose from 3 different bell sounds. The timer keeps running and bells keep firing even when your screen locks.

YOUR SETTINGS. YOUR PRESETS.
Dial in your rounds, work time, rest time, and warning timing. Save it as a preset. Next session, launch it in one tap. Build as many as you want.

KNOW YOUR NUMBERS
Every session is saved automatically — rounds completed, duration, workout name. A 365-day activity heatmap shows exactly how often you're putting in the work.

NOTHING IN THE WAY
No ads. No subscriptions. No account. No internet required. Dark screen, aggressive design, instant response. Built to stay out of the way while you train.
